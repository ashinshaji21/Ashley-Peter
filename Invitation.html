How It All Began

Our Story

🤎 ✦ 🤎

"The first time I saw Ashley was at my sister's housewarming. I still remember calling my sister about picking up food — and somehow, without even thinking twice, I decided to get food for Ashley too. It wasn't about trying to impress anyone; it was simply who I was, long before I realized how special she would become in my life.

We started talking on Facebook as two strangers, sharing simple conversations that slowly turned into something I began to look forward to every day. Eventually, I asked for her number, and those random chats became part of my routine.

One day while I was driving, I casually asked where she was — and when she said she was nearby, I decided to stop by just to say hi. That quick hello turned into a Starbucks coffee… a small moment that quietly meant more than either of us realized at the time.

Months later, almost out of nowhere, I finally asked her out. What started as a simple question became the beginning of our story — a journey that took us from strangers, to friends, to something far deeper.

And now, here we are… writing a forever that once began with a simple hello. 🤎

Questions & Answers

When is the RSVP deadline?▾

Please RSVP by March 15th, 2026 so we can have an accurate headcount for the celebration. We want to make sure everything is perfect for you!

Can I bring a date?▾

Please check your invite for your +1! If you have any questions about your invitation, feel free to reach out to us directly.

What will the weather be like?▾

Welcome to Kottayam, Kerala — where lush greenery meets tropical warmth! In June, expect a mix of sunshine and refreshing monsoon showers, with temperatures around 75–85°F (24–30°C). We recommend light, breathable outfits, comfortable footwear, and a small umbrella — just in case the Kerala rain decides to join our celebration. 🌧️🌿

Where should I park?▾

Both the church and Grand Arena have plenty of parking for guests. Please follow the venue signs and staff directions upon arrival. We also recommend using our shuttle bus service for a stress-free journey!

What should I wear?▾

Feel free to dress in traditional wear or formal attire. A suggested colour palette will be shared one month before the wedding — though it is not mandatory. Your presence is what matters most to us! 🌸

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?▾

Both the church ceremony and the reception will take place indoors, so you can enjoy the celebration comfortably regardless of the weather outside.

Whom should I contact with questions?▾

For any questions, you may contact Peter or Ashley directly. Closer to the wedding day, we will also share contact details of family members who can assist you during the event. You're in good hands! 🤎
